    About ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE Courier

    We are committed to delivering exceptional shipping services that are reliable, safe, and cost-effective. With over 29 years in the logistics industry, we have become a trusted name across five continents.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What type of cargo can ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE handle?
    We handle a wide range of cargo including documents, parcels, and pallets. We also specialise in the transportation of dangerous or hazardous goods with full regulatory compliance.
    Does ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE provide insurance for my shipment?
    Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance. Coverage terms apply — please consult our customer service team for a tailored policy to suit your shipment.
    What is the cost of shipping with ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE?
    Cost depends on weight, destination, mode of transportation, and shipment type. We offer competitive rates and provide customised quotes — contact us for an assessment.
    Does ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE provide real-time tracking?
    Yes — all shipments include real-time tracking. You can track via our website or by contacting our customer service team at any time.
    Can I change or cancel my shipment?
    Yes, changes or cancellations may be made. Applicable charges depend on the stage of shipment and the service selected. Contact us as early as possible.
    What if my shipment is lost or damaged?
    We take every precaution to ensure safe delivery. In the unlikely event of loss or damage, our comprehensive claims process ensures fair and timely compensation.
    Does ATLAS EXPRESS SERVICE handle customs clearance?
    Yes — our expert team ensures full customs compliance for every international shipment, making the clearance process smooth and hassle-free.
